Charcoal Interior Space

Pressed, vine, and pencil charcoal on paper, 11" x 14"


This piece features an interior space that shows it has been recently "lived in". The space shows a pillow on the floor, and a piece of clothing hanging out of the laundry hamper as if it was recently thrown in. The piece was completed with pressed, vine, and pencil charcoal on paper.
