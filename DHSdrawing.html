Drawing & Painting at Dumont High School

Drawing & Painting at Dumont High School, pencil on paper, acrylic paint on paper


These projects were completed during my Drawing & Painting course at Dumont High School. The course dealt with the projects on the basics of drawing, including shading and contour line drawing. We started with a contour line drawing of a Coca-Cola bottle, then followed with a contour line landscape and contour line hand drawing. We were then taught shading which we used to create a still-life landscape drawing. For our final project, we created a 3D collage out of various materials on a small 3" x 5" board, which we then replicated with an acrylic painting.
