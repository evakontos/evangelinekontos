Maison Rogue Spring 2020

Maison Rogue Spring 2020 sticker design, 2" x 2"

Link to Maison Rogue

Maison Rogue is a curated ecommerce concept for women's fashion and lifestyle. The company was created in 2018 by Aphrodite Comodromos and Loren O'Neill, who decided to leave their corporate fashion jobs to create their own lifestyle space that reflects the everyday high-low mix of fashion, beauty, and home. Aphrodite asked me to create a set of stickers for the Spring 2020 season, in conjunction with their lookbook "Destination Dreaming".
