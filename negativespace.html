Negative Space Still-Life

Pressed, vine, and pencil charcoal on paper, 11" x 14"


This piece features a still-life set up drawn in pressed, vine, and pencil charcoal on paper. The assignment was to complete a negative space still-life drawing, so I created positive space around the objects, erasing out the negative space. I used a kneaded eraser to create the still-life objects with negative space.
