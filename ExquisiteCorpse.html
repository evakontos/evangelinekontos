Exquisite Corpse

Emergent Interaction, Exquisite Corpse, 1500 x 1200px, Group Project


In this assignment, the goal was to employ emergence in creative media. Working together with a partner, we used our creativity to come up with an unexpected design. This concept, called "Exquisite Corpse", originated from the famous Surrealists Andre Baton, Yves Tanguy, Jacques Prevert, and Marcel Duchamp in order to bring an unknown element to design. I worked together with Vivienne Lee to generate a set of rules we would implement in our piece. We decided to diverge from the basic "corpse" layout and utilized our rules to create a landscape. Our final design produced a more natural landscape setting, combining both elements of vector illustration and photography with an indentical color palette to allow cohesion.


Rules:

6 cells, number order is in Z formation (reading left to right: 1, 2, 4, 3, 5, 6)

Vivienne: odd cells, Evangeline: even cells

Dimensions of Cells:

1125 x 400

375 x 400

750 x 400

landscape/environment theme: sky, land, underwater

identical color palette to create a cohesive look

any style of graphics are allowed

the design will not be randomized, we will be able to view each other's additions to the exquisite corpse throughout the process
