BU Hellenic Cultural Society Apparel

Binghamton University Hellenic Cultural Society Merch Spring 2021

Link to Custom Ink Fundraiser

As Vice President of Binghamton University's Hellenic Cultural Society and Graphic Designer, I was in charge of designing T-shirts for our annual club fundraiser. Generally, this fundraiser is meant to generate extra funds for our club and the designed apparel is meant to be worn by members at the annual Greek Independence Day Parade in NYC that we participate in. I came up with multiple concepts for the apparel, mainly including Greek statue outlines and evil eye concepts. I then mocked up the designs on T-shirts, hoodies, and long sleeve T's in a variety of color options. The final design was voted on and chosen by the general E-Board. I set up and organized the fundraiser using Custom Ink to sell the final T-shirt design to members and their family & friends. I created a flyer to market the fundraiser across Instagram and Facebook platforms.
