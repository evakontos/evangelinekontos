Duct Tape Shoe Replica

Duct Tape Shoe Replica, duct tape and cardboard


For my final project for my Sculpture 1 Course at Binghamton University, the assignment was to recreate a pair of shoes purely out of duct tape. The shoe was meant to represent the creators personality. I chose a pair of black open-back snake patterned heels, which I felt exuded the feminine, chic component of my personality. I certainly prioritize my clothes and fashion, and love to wear things that make me feel stylish and powerful. The shoe replicas were created out of multiple layers of duct tape-- approximately 1.5 rolls worth. A small piece of cardboard was used in the heels for stability.
